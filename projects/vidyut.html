Vidyut

VIDYUT represents an innovative approach to electric vehicle design, focusing on lightweight construction and aerodynamic efficiency. With a kerb weight of just 99 kg and a drag coefficient of 0.23, VIDYUT showcases the potential for high performance and energy efficiency in electric prototypes. The use of carbon fibre in its shell material enhances both strength and weight reduction, contributing to its impressive efficiency of 70 km/kWh. Despite its lightweight design, VIDYUT maintains stability and durability, making it a promising contender in the realm of electric vehicle prototypes.

  • Category: Prototype
  • Kerb Weight: 99 kg
  • Shell Material: Carbon Fibre
  • Drag Coefficient: 0.23
  • Number of Wheels: 3
  • Efficiency: 70 km/kWh
